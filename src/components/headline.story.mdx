# Headline

Headline component is used for providing titles with paragraphs on graphical elements and photos.

Headline can be positioned absolutely in relation to its parent.

## Basic usage with properties

### Default

Just default variant without any additional functionality.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Default">
    <Headline title="This is title" paragraph="Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet" />
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### Breakpoint Coordinates

Suppose to position headline absolutely to its parent.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="With Breakpoint Coordinates">
    <Headline title="This is title" paragraph="Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet" />
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### Breakpoint Width

Maintain width based on breakpoint.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="With Breakpoint Width">
    <Headline title="This is title" paragraph="Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet" />
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Additional functionality

### With Gloom

Gloom component aas a child suppose to make shadow below the headline.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="With Gloom">
    <Headline title="This is title" paragraph="Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet">
      <Gloom />
    </Headline>
  </Story>
</Canvas>
